About Pinnacle Shield Insurance

Established in 1985, Pinnacle Shield Insurance has built a strong reputation as a reliable provider of personal and family insurance for more than 40 years. Beginning as a small office in Hartford, Connecticut, the company has expanded into a nationwide insurer serving customers across all 50 states.

Pinnacle Shield, the goal is to make insurance straightforward, clear, and cost-effective. The company uses modern technology to simplify the quote process and help customers better understand their coverage options. Licensed agents and data specialists collaborate to provide competitive pricing and a smooth, stress-free claims experience.>

Throughout its history, Pinnacle Shield has helped protect over 2 million families and handled more than $5 billion in claims. Its dedication to customer service has earned the company an A+ rating from the Better Business Bureau, along with recognition as one of New England’s leading insurance providers
