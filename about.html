About Pinnacle Shield Insurance

Founded in 1985, Pinnacle Shield Insurance has been a trusted name in personal and family insurance for over four decades. What started as a small office in Hartford, Connecticut has grown into a nationwide provider serving customers in all 50 states.

At Pinnacle Shield, we believe that insurance should be simple, transparent, and affordable. That's why we invest in technology that makes getting a quote fast and understanding your coverage easy. Our team of licensed agents and data analysts work together to ensure our pricing is competitive and our claims process is hassle-free.

Over the years, we've protected more than 2 million families and processed over $5 billion in claims. Our commitment to customer satisfaction has earned us an A+ rating from the Better Business Bureau and recognition as one of the top insurance providers in New England.
