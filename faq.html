Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our insurance products and services.

Pinnacle Shield Insurance offers three main types of personal insurance: Auto Insurance for vehicles including cars, trucks, and SUVs; Home Insurance for homeowners covering structural damage, personal property, and liability; and Life Insurance providing financial protection for your loved ones with both term and whole life options. Each type offers Basic, Standard, and Premium coverage tiers to fit your needs and budget.

Your premium is calculated using a combination of factors specific to each insurance type. For auto insurance, we consider your age, vehicle details, annual mileage, and driving record. For home insurance, factors include your home's value, age, construction type, and installed safety features. Life insurance premiums are based on age, health factors, lifestyle choices, and the coverage amount you select. Our online quote tool shows you a detailed breakdown of exactly how each factor affects your rate — complete transparency, no surprises.

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your coverage level at any time during your policy period. If you upgrade from Basic to Standard or Premium, the change takes effect immediately and your premium will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Contact our customer service team or log into your online account to make changes.

Several factors influence your auto insurance premium. Your age plays a role — drivers under 25 and over 65 typically pay more due to statistical risk profiles. Vehicle age matters because newer cars cost more to repair or replace. Annual mileage affects your rate since more time on the road increases the likelihood of an incident. Your driving record is significant — a clean record earns the best rates, while tickets and accidents increase premiums. Finally, the coverage level you choose (Basic, Standard, or Premium) determines the breadth of protection and directly impacts cost.

Yes! Having a monitored security system qualifies you for a 5% discount on your home insurance premium. Additionally, if your home has fire sprinklers, you can receive an 8% discount. These discounts can be combined, so a home with both a security system and fire sprinklers could save up to 13% on monthly premiums. We encourage homeowners to invest in safety features — they protect your family and save you money.

Basic coverage provides essential protection at the lowest cost. It covers the core risks — liability for auto, structural damage for home, and a standard death benefit for life insurance. Standard coverage is our most popular tier and includes everything in Basic plus broader protection like comprehensive and collision for auto, personal property replacement for home, and higher benefit limits for life. Premium coverage is our most comprehensive tier, adding benefits like rental car coverage, full replacement value, increased liability limits, and priority claims processing. Premium costs approximately 40% more than Standard but provides the most complete peace of mind.

Filing a claim with Pinnacle Shield is straightforward. You can file a claim 24/7 through three channels: Online — log into your account at pinnacleshield.com and navigate to the Claims section; Phone — call our claims hotline at 1-800-555-CLAIM; or Mobile App — use our app to submit photos and documentation directly from your phone. After filing, a dedicated claims adjuster will be assigned to your case within 24 hours. Most claims are processed within 5–10 business days, and Premium customers receive priority processing.
